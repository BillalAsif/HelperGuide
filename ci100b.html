CI-100B

Code Description
0101 Abnormal rotation of drive motor for feeding belt (SMA01)

How to enter RAS

  1. Button 1 will increment the display figure in multiples of 10.
  2. Button 2 increments the figure by 1.
  3. Button 3 will enter/start the command.
  4. Press buttons 1&2 together to clear the command.
  5. Operate buttons 1&2 to display the code FF.
  6. Press button 3 and Co00 should be displayed.

Operational Cover

Firstly, use the yellow dot key to unlock and remove the Collection Box. Next use the red dot key to unlock and rack out the main machine.

Step 1

Undo the 2 screws indicated and remove the metal cover.

Step 2

With the cover removed, unplug connectors K11, 12 & 13.

Step 3

The cover is secured in place by 4 screws; 1 on the left-hand side.

Step 4

2 on the right-hand side.

Step 5

1 on the front of the machine.

Step 6

Slide the cover forward & off.

Feed Unit

The Operational Cover Unit must be removed first.

Step 1

On the left-hand side, loosen 2 screws and push up tension plate to loosen the belt then remove it.

Step 2

On the right-hand side, undo the 2 screws indicated and remove the CPU board cover.

Step 3

Above the CBU board, carefully undo the 2 screws and remove the metal silver bracket.

Step 4

Remove the black screw and the USB restraining bracket.

Step 5

Unplug the micro-USB connector U1 and the power connector J2

Step 6

Open the 7 cable clamps and unplug the connectors A1 & A2.

Step 7

Pull A1, A2 cable looms, out of the cable clamps.

Step 8

Undo the 2 screws indicated on the right-hand side.

Step 9

Then undo the 2 screws on the left-hand side.

Step 10

Undo the clamp that secures the top of the BV in place as this makes the removal of the feed unit easier.

Step 11

Lift the feed unit clear of the machine, do not to damage A1, A2 cable looms on the right-hand side.

BV Unit

Step 1

Unplug connector C7 from the right-hand side of the unit.

Step 2

Undo the screw indicated and release the locking bar.

Step 3

Undo the 2 black screws on the right-hand side.

Step 4

Undo the single black screw on the left-hand side of the unit.

Step 5

Carefully lift and remove the unit from the machine.

Stacker Units

Please note: After the stackers have been removed, any stacker that contain cash, must be fitted in its original location.

Step 1

Remove the black screw and the USB restraining bracket.

Step 2

Unplug the micro-USB connector U1 and the power connector J2.

Step 3

Remove 3 black screws, move the cable and plate out of the way.

Step 4

Remove 2 black screws and move metal plate from the machine.

Step 5

Unplug the 6 cable connectors G11, G12, G21, G22, G31 & G32.

Step 6

Undo the 5 black screws and remove the stacker fixing plate.

Step 7

On the left-hand side undo the 3 black screws and the 3 plates.

Step 8

Remove the 6 screws indicated that secure the stackers in place.

Step 9

Pull out stacker 1 first from the rear, then 2, lastly 3.

Transport Unit

Prior to removing the Transport Unit, it is necessary to remove the following units:
- The Collection Box
- Operational Cover Unit
- Feed Transport Unit
- Recognition Unit (BV)
- All Stacker Units
Unlike other CI products, the CI-5B only has one transport unit as opposed separate upper and lower transport units. Before removing the transport unit, it is advised to take a photo of the cable routing to the CPU board to assist when refitting the cables.

Step 1

The first step is to loosen the 2 screws indicated and the tensioner bracket will drop allowing the timing belt to be removed. Then carefully unplug connector J11.

Step 2

On the right-hand side, open all the white and black cable clamps that secure the cable looms. Unplug all the connectors and the connector indicated in yellow.

On the right-hand side of the machine, the motor cover needs to be removed. This cover is secured in place by 3 screws.

Step 3

1 at the rear and 1 on the top.

Step 4

1 at the left-hand side. 3 screws and remove the cover

The Transport Unit is secured in place by 4 black screws.

Step 5

One at the front left-hand side.

Step 6

One at the front right-hand side.

Step 7

One at the rear left-hand side.

Step 8

One at the rear right-hand side.

With the 4 screws removed, it is now possible to lift the Transport Unit free from the chassis take care not to snag any of the cables that come away with the unit.
