How to enter RAS

  1. Pull out machine, open CPU cover and switch off machine
  2. Set dip 1 up on switch pack 2 and power on machine
  3. Wait unit FF is displayed, use button 1 & 2 to set Ras command
  4. Button 3 to execute

Operational Cover

Use the Red Dot Key to open the left-hand side door.

Step 1

Undo the 2 screws indicated.Remove the small metal cover.

Step 2

Remove the 4 screws and lift the metal top and side cover clear of the machine.

Step 3

On right-hand side, unplug connector CNSL. Remove the screws indicated.

Step 4

On the left-hand side, unplug connector CNL11. Remove 2 screws. Manually release and open the cassette door.

Step 5

Operate the manual latch to unlock the Outlet Shutter so that it can be lifted, located behind red square.

Step 6

With the Outlet Shutter Lifted, remove the Operational Cover in the direction shown. Shutter stays in place.

Feed Unit

The Operational Cover Unit must be removed first.

Step 1

On the left-hand side, unplug connectors CNA11 & CNS11 and undo 1 screw.

Step 2

On the right-hand side, unset connectors CNA21 & CNS21 and 2 screws.

Step 3

Next, remove the timing belt from the timing gear, no need to fully remove the belt from the machine.

Step 4

Next, remove the timing belt from the timing gear, no need to fully remove the belt from the machine.

BV Unit

Step 1

Firstly, remove the Operational Cover Unit as previously described. On the right-hand, unplug connector CNIF. Undo and remove the screw indicated and then rotate the lock bracket clockwise. Undo the silver screw that secures the earth cable and 2 black screws.

Step 2

On the left-hand side of the machine, undo and remove the screw that secures the earth cable. Undo and remove the single black screw indicated. Carefully lift the BV upwards and clear of the machine.

Upper Transport Unit

Operational unit, feed unit and BV must be removed first.

Step 1

Unplug connectors CNB11, CNP11 & CNB21, open cable clamps where required.

Step 2

There are 4 black screws securing the Upper Transport Unit in place. 3 of these accessible from the top.

Step 3

The 4th screw is situated on the left-hand side of the machine, in the lower section – once removed, the unit can be lifted clear.

Stacker Unit
Step 1

Open the door & press down on the release latch and gently pull out the stacker unit halfway.

Step 2

Unplug the connectors CNG11 and CNG21 on the right-hand side. Place cables away from module.

Step 3

On the left-hand side of the machine, operate the lock lever and remove the stacker unit completely.

To separate stackers, follow steps below.

Step 4

Undo the 3 screws indicated and remove the bracket.

Step 5

Each stacker has 1 screw on the left hand-side.

Step 6

And 3 screws on the right-hand side.

Step 7

Each one has 3 plugs that needs to be disconnected.
